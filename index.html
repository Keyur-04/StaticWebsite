Our Story

Founded with a passion for people, Lifeline Multispeciality Hospital began as a small healthcare center and has grown into a trusted name in advanced medical care. Rooted in compassion and excellence, we believe everyone deserves healing that’s both affordable and human-centered.

Our Mission

At Lifeline Multispeciality Hospital, our mission is to provide ethical, accessible, and high-quality healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors. We strive to combine advanced medical technology with a human touch, ensuring that every treatment is personalized, safe, and effective.

Our Vision

To be a leading healthcare institution known for trust, innovation, and compassionate healing.

Our Services

At Lifeline Multispeciality Hospital, we offer a wide range of healthcare services designed to meet the needs of individuals and families at every stage of life. Our team of skilled doctors, nurses, and specialists work together to provide personalized, high-quality care across multiple departments.

24/7 Emergency & Trauma Care

Immediate care for critical injuries, accidents, and urgent health conditions.

General Medicine & Internal Medicine

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of medical conditions.

Cardiology

Advanced heart care services including ECG, ECHO, stress tests, and expert consultations.

Orthopedics

Treatment for bone, joint, and muscle conditions — from fractures to joint replacements.

Gynecology & Obstetrics

Women’s health services, including pregnancy care, deliveries, and fertility support.

ICU & Critical Care

Fully equipped intensive care units with round-the-clock monitoring and support.

Neurology

Diagnosis and management of brain, spine, and nerve-related disorders.

Pharmacy Services

In-house pharmacy with genuine, prescribed medicines available 24/7.

General & Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive surgeries for faster recovery and better outcomes.
